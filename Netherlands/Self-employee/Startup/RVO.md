# Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO)

## What is RVO?

**RVO** stands for **Rijksdienst voor Ondernemend Nederland** (Netherlands Enterprise Agency). It is a government agency that operates under the Ministry of Economic Affairs and Climate Policy.

## Role and Mission

RVO is the Dutch government's primary implementation agency for entrepreneurship, innovation, sustainability, and international business cooperation. The agency's main mission is to help entrepreneurs and organizations succeed in sustainable business development both within the Netherlands and internationally.

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. **Business Support**
- Providing information and guidance to entrepreneurs
- Managing subsidy and grant programs
- Supporting innovation and research initiatives
- Facilitating international business expansion

### 2. **Startup Visa Program**
- **Maintaining the official list of recognized facilitators** for the Startup Visa program
- **Assessing business plans** submitted by startup visa applicants
- **Evaluating innovation criteria** to determine if a startup meets requirements
- **Advising the IND (Immigration and Naturalisation Service)** on visa applications

### 3. **Sustainability and Energy**
- Supporting energy transition projects
- Managing environmental subsidy schemes
- Promoting circular economy initiatives

### 4. **Agriculture and Horticulture**
- Supporting agri-food innovation
- Managing agricultural subsidy programs

## RVO's Role in Your Startup Visa Application

When you apply for a Netherlands Startup Visa, RVO plays a **critical assessment role**:

### Assessment Criteria
RVO evaluates your application based on:

1. **Innovation Level**
   - Is your product/service new to the Netherlands market?
   - Does it incorporate new technology?
   - Does it involve innovative organizational approaches?

2. **Business Viability**
   - Quality and feasibility of your business plan
   - Market potential and scalability
   - Financial projections

3. **Economic Value**
   - Potential job creation
   - Contribution to Dutch economy
   - Investment potential

4. **Facilitator Credibility**
   - Verification that your facilitator is recognized
   - Assessment of the support agreement

### Process Flow
```
Your Application → IND receives → RVO assesses → RVO advises IND → IND decides
```

The entire process takes up to **90 days**, with RVO's assessment being a crucial component.

## How RVO Differs from IND

It's important to understand the distinction:

| Aspect | RVO | IND |
|--------|-----|-----|
| **Parent Ministry** | Economic Affairs | Justice and Security |
| **Primary Role** | Business & economic development | Immigration control |
| **In Visa Process** | Assesses business viability & innovation | Makes final visa decision |
| **What They Check** | Business plan, innovation, economic value | Identity, security, legal requirements |
| **Decision Power** | Advisory (recommends to IND) | Final (grants or denies visa) |

## Tips for Working with RVO

### During Application
✅ **DO:**
- Ensure your business plan clearly demonstrates innovation
- Show specific benefits to the Dutch economy
- Provide detailed market analysis for the Netherlands/Europe
- Work closely with your facilitator on the plan

❌ **DON'T:**
- Submit generic business plans meant for other markets
- Underestimate the innovation requirement
- Copy existing business models without clear differentiation
- Rush the business plan - quality matters significantly

### After Approval
RVO continues to be relevant:
- They manage various subsidy programs you can apply for
- They provide ongoing business support services
- They can help with scaling and internationalization

## Additional RVO Programs for Entrepreneurs

Once you're established in the Netherlands, you can benefit from:

### Tax Benefits
- **WBSO (R&D Tax Credit):** Tax deduction for R&D activities
- **Innovation Box:** Reduced tax rate on innovation-based profits

### Grants and Loans
- **Innovation Credit:** Loans for innovative projects
- **SME+ Innovation Fund:** Funding for SME innovation
- **Early-Stage Financing:** Support for startups (€75M program)

### Knowledge and Networks
- **Top Sectors Programs:** Sector-specific innovation initiatives
- **TKI (Topsector Knowledge and Innovation):** Public-private partnerships
- **Innovation Attachés:** Network of tech experts in Dutch embassies worldwide

## Important Notes

⚠️ **RVO is not the decision-maker** for your visa - that's IND's role. However, RVO's assessment carries significant weight in the decision.

⚠️ **Innovation is key** - RVO's primary focus is determining whether your startup is truly innovative and valuable to the Dutch economy.

⚠️ **Business plan quality matters** - RVO employs experienced business assessors who will critically evaluate your plan.
